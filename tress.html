About Trees

Trees are essential for our planet's well-being. They provide oxygen, shade, and habitat for wildlife. There are thousands of tree species around the world, each with its unique characteristics and benefits.

Types of Trees

There are various types of trees, including deciduous and evergreen trees. Deciduous trees shed their leaves in the fall, while evergreen trees retain their leaves year-round.
